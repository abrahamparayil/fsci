FREE SOFTWARE COMMUNITY OF INDIA

Free Software Community of India (FSCI) intends to act as an umbrella community to connect various disconnected Free Software communities in India and to co-ordinate Free Software related activities across India.

FSCI Wiki

FSCI maintains a MediaWiki instance at wiki.fsci.org.in which acts as a 'decentralised central resource' as every one gets to fill in their parts and one could find all parts in one place. FSCI Wiki

Git Repository Hosting

FSCI runs a free instance of GitLab Community Edition at git.fosscommunity.in. GitLab is a powerful platform for collaborative software development. Git Repository
